Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This notice explains how TAGGLE handles your personal information.

Introduction

We collect and process your personal information when you use our platform for booking flights, hotels, or cabs. Your data is handled securely and only for legitimate purposes to provide you with a smooth booking experience.

1. Legal basis for processing your information

We process personal information under applicable data protection laws such as for fulfilling services, public interest, legitimate business needs, and compliance with legal obligations.

2. What information we collect

We collect Personal Information such as your name, contact details, email address, travel preferences,payment information, and any details you provide while using our services. This may also include technical data such as IP address, device information, browser type, and interaction logs on our platform.

3. How we collect your information

We collect information directly from you when you use our website, booking forms, customer support, or while interacting with our services. We may also collect data automatically through cookies, analytics tools, and third-party service providers.

4. How we use your information

Your information is used to complete bookings, manage your travel, personalize your experience, provide customer support, send updates, improve our services, and comply with legal or security requirements.

5. Retention of your information

We retain your Personal Information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy—typically for regulatory, operational, and legal requirements. Once no longer needed, your data is securely deleted or anonymized.

6. How we ensure data security

Your information is protected through encryption, secure servers, restricted access, and continuous monitoring. We follow industry-standard security measures to prevent unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, or exposure of your data.

7. Your data rights

You have the right to access, update, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your Personal Information. You may also request data portability or withdraw consent at any time, subject to applicable laws.

8. Information regarding minors

Our services are not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect Personal Information from minors. If data is collected accidentally, it will be deleted promptly upon request.

9. Transfer of data overseas

Your information may be processed or stored in servers located outside your country. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and that your data remains protected at all times.

10. Links to external websites

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. Please review their policies before sharing any information.
